General Health Questionnaire (GHQ-12)

Please answer how you have been feeling recently.

Question 1 of 12
Scoring (Likert): 0–3 per item. For negatively phrased items: Not at all = 0, No more than usual = 1, Rather more than usual = 2, Much more than usual = 3. For positively phrased items (ability/well-being), the scale is reversed: Better than usual = 0, Same as usual = 1, Less than usual = 2, Much less than usual = 3.
Screening tool only, not a diagnosis. If you’re in crisis, seek immediate help.
